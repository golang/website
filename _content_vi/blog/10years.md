Chuc mung sinh nhat, Go!

Cuoi tuan nay, chung ta ky niem 10 nam ke tu
[ban phat hanh Go](https://opensource.googleblog.com/2009/11/hey-ho-lets-go.html),
danh dau cot moc Go tron 10 tuoi voi tu cach la mot ngon ngu lap trinh ma nguon mo
va mot he sinh thai de xay dung phan mem mang hien dai.

Nhan dip nay,
[Renee French](https://twitter.com/reneefrench),
nguoi tao ra
[Go gopher](/blog/gopher),
da ve buc tranh day thu vi nay:

<a href="10years/gopher10th-large.jpg">
{{image "10years/gopher10th-small.jpg" 850}}
</a>

Ky niem 10 nam cua Go khien toi nho lai dau thang 11 nam 2009,
khi chung toi dang chuan bi gioi thieu Go voi the gioi.
Chung toi khong biet se nhan duoc phan ung nhu the nao,
lieu co ai quan tam den ngon ngu nho be nay hay khong.
Toi hy vong rang ngay ca khi khong ai su dung Go,
thi it nhat chung toi cung se lam noi bat mot so y tuong hay,
dac biet la cach Go tiep can dong thoi va interface,
de co the anh huong den cac ngon ngu ra doi sau do.

Khi da ro rang moi nguoi thuc su hao hung voi Go,
toi tim hieu lich su cua nhung ngon ngu pho bien
nhu C, C++, Perl, Python va Ruby,
de xem mat bao lau moi ngon ngu moi dat duoc su pho bien rong rai.
Vi du, trong suy nghi cua toi, Perl co ve nhu xuat hien voi hinh hai hoan chinh
vao giua den cuoi nhung nam 1990, cung cac CGI script va web,
nhung thuc ra no duoc phat hanh lan dau vao nam 1987.
Mau hinh nay lap lai o gan nhu moi ngon ngu toi xem qua:
co ve nhu can xap xi mot thap ky cai tien ben bi, am tham
va pho bien rong rai truoc khi mot ngon ngu moi that su cat canh.

Toi tu hoi: sau mot thap ky, Go se dung o dau?

Hom nay, chung ta da co cau tra loi cho cau hoi do:
Go co mat o khap noi, duoc it nhat [mot trieu lap trinh vien tren toan the gioi](https://research.swtch.com/gophercount) su dung.

Muc tieu ban dau cua Go la co so ha tang he thong mang,
dieu ma ngay nay chung ta goi la phan mem dam may.
Ngay nay, moi nha cung cap dam may lon deu su dung ha tang dam may cot loi duoc viet bang Go,
nhu Docker, Etcd, Istio, Kubernetes, Prometheus va Terraform;
phan lon cac
[du an cua Cloud Native Computing Foundation](https://www.cncf.io/projects/)
duoc viet bang Go.
Vo so cong ty cung dang su dung Go de dua cong viec cua ho len dam may,
tu cac startup xay dung moi tu dau
den cac doanh nghiep dang hien dai hoa ngan xep phan mem.
Go cung da duoc ung dung vuot xa muc tieu dam may ban dau,
voi cac truong hop su dung trai dai
tu
viec dieu khien nhung he thong nhung sieu nho bang
[GoBot](https://gobot.io) va [TinyGo](https://tinygo.org/)
den phat hien ung thu bang
[phan tich du lieu lon quy mo lon va hoc may tai GRAIL](https://medium.com/grail-eng/bigslice-a-cluster-computing-system-for-go-7e03acd2419b),
va moi thu o giua hai dau do.

Tat ca nhung dieu nay cho thay Go da thanh cong vuot xa moi mong doi tao bao nhat cua chung toi.
Va thanh cong cua Go khong chi nam o ngon ngu.
No nam o ngon ngu, he sinh thai, va dac biet la cong dong cung nhau hop tac.

Nam 2009, ngon ngu nay la mot y tuong hay cung mot ban phac thao dang hoat dong.
Lenh `go` khi do chua ton tai:
chung toi chay nhung lenh nhu `6g` de bien dich va `6l` de lien ket tep nhi phan,
duoc tu dong hoa bang makefile.
Chung toi go dau cham phay o cuoi cau lenh.
Toan bo chuong trinh dung lai trong qua trinh garbage collection,
va bo gom rac khi do cung kho tan dung tot hai nhan xu ly.
Go chi chay tren Linux va Mac, tren x86 32 bit va 64 bit va ARM 32 bit.

Trong suot thap ky qua, voi su giup do cua cac lap trinh vien Go tren khap the gioi,
chung toi da phat trien y tuong va ban phac thao nay thanh mot ngon ngu nang suat cao
voi he thong cong cu tuyet voi,
mot trinh trien khai dat chat luong san xuat,
mot
[bo gom rac hien dai hang dau](/blog/ismmkeynote),
va [cac ban port toi 12 he dieu hanh va 10 kien truc](/doc/install/source#introduction).

Bat ky ngon ngu lap trinh nao cung can su ho tro cua mot he sinh thai phat trien manh.
Ban phat hanh ma nguon mo la hat giong cho he sinh thai do,
nhung ke tu do, rat nhieu nguoi da dong gop thoi gian va tai nang cua minh
de boi dap he sinh thai Go bang nhung huong dan, sach, khoa hoc, bai viet blog,
podcast, cong cu, tich hop, va tat nhien la cac goi Go co the tai lai bang `go` `get`.
Go khong the nao thanh cong neu khong co su ho tro cua he sinh thai nay.

Tat nhien, he sinh thai lai can su ho tro cua mot cong dong phat trien manh.
Trong nam 2019 co hang chuc hoi nghi Go tren khap the gioi,
cung voi
[hon 150 nhom meetup Go voi hon 90.000 thanh vien](https://www.meetup.com/pro/go).
[GoBridge](https://golangbridge.org)
va
[Women Who Go](https://medium.com/@carolynvs/www-loves-gobridge-ccb26309f667)
giup dua nhung tieng noi moi vao cong dong Go,
thong qua co van, dao tao, va hoc bong hoi nghi.
Chi rieng nam nay, ho da huan luyen
hang tram nguoi thuoc nhung nhom truoc day it duoc dai dien
thong qua cac buoi workshop, noi thanh vien cong dong day va co van cho nhung nguoi moi den voi Go.

Hien co
[hon mot trieu lap trinh vien Go](https://research.swtch.com/gophercount)
tren toan the gioi,
va cac cong ty tren khap dia cau dang tim cach tuyen them.
Thuc te, moi nguoi thuong noi voi chung toi rang viec hoc Go
da giup ho co duoc nhung cong viec dau tien trong nganh cong nghe.
Sau cung, dieu chung toi tu hao nhat ve Go
khong phai la mot tinh nang duoc thiet ke tot hay mot doan ma thong minh
ma la tac dong tich cuc ma Go da mang lai cho cuoc song cua rat nhieu nguoi.
Chung toi huong den viec tao ra mot ngon ngu giup chung toi tro thanh nhung lap trinh vien gioi hon,
va chung toi vo cung phan khoi khi Go da giup duoc rat nhieu nguoi khac.

Khi
[\#GoTurns10](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23GoTurns10),
toi hy vong moi nguoi se danh it phut de cung ky niem
cong dong Go va tat ca nhung gi chung ta da dat duoc.
Thay mat toan bo nhom Go tai Google,
xin cam on tat ca moi nguoi da dong hanh cung chung toi trong suot thap ky vua qua.
Hay bien thap ky tiep theo tro nen tuyet voi hon nua!
